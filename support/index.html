Support

Wayn Cafe helps people choose cafes in Kuwait by practical need, area, and vibe.

For help, feedback, correction requests, privacy questions, or store-review contact, email support@wayn-kw.com.

What to include when contacting support

  • the device type, such as iPhone or Android,
  • app version if available,
  • what you were trying to do,
  • what happened,
  • screenshots if helpful and if they do not contain private information.

Cafe information corrections

The first store-candidate version uses a conservative static cafe snapshot. If you notice a cafe name, area, status, or practical detail that needs review, send it through support. Candidate corrections should be treated as review requests, not automatic public changes.

Privacy questions

For privacy questions or requests, contact support and include “Privacy” in the message subject/body.

Account deletion

The first static-snapshot release does not include production accounts. If a future release adds accounts, this page and the Privacy Policy will be updated with the account deletion or data removal process.
